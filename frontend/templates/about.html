About GastroDetect AI

An intelligent, accessible platform for early gastric cancer detection in Kenya and beyond.

Why This Project Matters

In Kenya, many gastric cancer cases are diagnosed too late, leading to high mortality rates. The disease is often asymptomatic in early stages and diagnostic tools are limited in rural hospitals.

  • Over 3,000 Kenyans are diagnosed with gastric cancer annually.
  • Late diagnosis is responsible for up to 80% mortality in advanced cases.
  • Endoscopy services are often limited to major urban hospitals.
  • This project aims to bring AI-based diagnosis closer to frontline clinicians.

Our Vision

To democratize access to early cancer detection by leveraging intelligent, affordable, and explainable AI tools.

Our Mission

To assist medical professionals in low-resource settings with instant diagnostic support using image-based deep learning models.

How the Model Works

Our system uses a fine-tuned ResNet50 Convolutional Neural Network trained on labeled endoscopic images from the Kvasir dataset.

  • Uploads are analyzed in real-time.
  • The model outputs a prediction (cancerous / non-cancerous).
  • A Grad-CAM heatmap shows areas the model focused on.
  • Clinicians can give feedback to improve future retraining.
