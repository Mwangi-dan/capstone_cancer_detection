About GastroDetect AI

GastroDetect AI is a smart medical platform built to assist clinicians and researchers in detecting gastric cancer using advanced deep learning techniques. It analyzes endoscopic images to identify cancerous lesions early, improving the chances of treatment success and survival.

How It Works

  • Users upload an endoscopic image via the prediction page.
  • The system uses a trained Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to analyze the image.
  • It returns a prediction (cancerous or non-cancerous) along with a confidence score.
  • Optionally, a Grad-CAM visualization shows the regions the model focused on during prediction.
  • All results are rendered instantly in a clean and interactive interface.
